RECIPES

Oatmeal with Berries
Ingredients: 1 cup rolled oats, 1.5 cups almond milk, 1/2 cup mixed berries, honey
Instructions: Cook oats with almond milk for 5min, top with berries and honey.

Grilled Chicken Salad
Ingredients: 100g grilled chicken breast, salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, carrot, olive oil, lemon juice
Instructions: Combine all, top with chicken and drizzle with olive oil + lemon juice.
